Clients wanted a lower-risk entry point. The 'Per Square Meter' approach enables scalable, transparent entry with high retention.

Risks & Mitigation

Inventory Damage

Fix: Asset tracking systems & dedicated maintenance replacement budgets built into margins.

Peak Season Overload

Fix: Pre-scheduling core holidays months in advance & maintaining a strong freelance installer network.

Early Acquisition

Fix: Focus heavily on securing 2-3 anchor clients initially to establish credibility & case studies.

In-House Teams

Fix: Emphasize our full-service convenience (design + storage), highly out-competing internal DIY costs.
